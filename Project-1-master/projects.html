Unsize: Make Fashion Work for You

Unsize

×

Unsize is a fashion experience company that makes fashion work for you. The nunchucks (what generate is making and calling it) is a fun way to measure yourself that doesn't show numbers. It processes them and spits them out as “size(yourname)” through our chrome extension so when you shop online at your favorite stores, you hit, “size (yourname)” and get clothing tailored to you. We are lead by design and by human needs and make money through retail partnerships.

Solving the problem: In a world where technology helps personalize experiences, buying clothing is still dated and boxes people into standards that don't always represent how they want to express.

Design Thinking in Education

Scout Labs

×
Scout Labs is a multi-disciplinary team (engineers, designers, business people etc.) of curious students who believe in the power of design for social good. Through the human-centered design process, the team (which includes you) defines a problem, determines the user of the problem, brainstorms solutions, prototypes solutions and tests with users to make the idea stronger. The process is about empathizing with users, embracing the unknown, and collaborating as a team to learn from failure and make something that matters.
